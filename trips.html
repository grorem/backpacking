Next trip:

Stage: Insurrection fomenting
Time: August 2013
Class:Grizzly Adams
Place: The Winds, Wyoming
The Winds, or really the Wind River mountain range in Western Wyoming, has much to offer us in the way of backpacking. It simply cannot all be explored in even a lifetime. This trip will be a 4-5 day, 23.4 mile loop starting at the Big Sandy trailhead at 42* 41' 16.98" N 109* 16' 11.76" W, going into an area known as the cirque of the towers, and then looping back around behind to the first campsite.
